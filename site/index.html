GCC Through Time

Aerial photographs and maps of the Glendale Community College campus and the Verdugo foothills, aligned across a century. Drag the slider to watch the campus appear, the streets fill in, and the freeway cut through the hills. Tap the ◉ markers for stories.

What you see by default is a reconstruction: the historical photographs are black-and-white; their colors here are transferred from today's imagery, gaps are filled from neighboring years, and the ~1900 view is rebuilt from 1928 photography guided by the 1900 map. Use the Original imagery toggle to see the untouched scans. Historical imagery courtesy of the UCSB Library Geospatial Collection; USGS topographic maps and USDA NAIP orthoimagery (public domain).
