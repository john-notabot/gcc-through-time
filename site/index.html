GCC Through Time

Aerial photographs and maps of the Glendale Community College campus and the Verdugo foothills, aligned across a century. Drag the slider to watch the campus appear, the streets fill in, and the freeway cut through the hills. Tap the ◉ markers for stories.

Historical imagery courtesy of the UCSB Library Geospatial Collection; USGS topographic maps and USDA NAIP orthoimagery (public domain). The 1971 view is a 1968–71 composite where the 1971 flight left a gap. Historical frames are aligned to the modern street grid to within a few meters; hillsides may shift slightly between eras.
